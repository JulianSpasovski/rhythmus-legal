What to Include

To help understand the issue, include as much of the following as you are comfortable sharing:

  • Device model
  • iOS version
  • Rhythmus app version and build number
  • A short description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or exported files, if helpful and if you choose to share them

There is no contact form on this site. Please avoid sending sensitive information unless it is necessary for support.
